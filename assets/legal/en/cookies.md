# Cookie Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0 — May 5, 2026
*(Web only. Mobile app does not use browser cookies.)*

## 1. What is a cookie?
A small text file stored on your device when you visit a website, helping us remember you across sessions.

## 2. Cookies we use

| Type | Purpose | Duration | Can disable? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Essential** | Login session, booking cart, CSRF | Session / 7 days | No (required to run) |
| **Preference** | Remember language, dark mode | 1 year | Yes |
| **Analytics** | Google Analytics 4 — traffic measurement | 14 months | **Yes** (consent banner) |
| **Marketing** | Meta Pixel, Google Ads (EU/US only) | 90 days | **Yes** (consent banner) |

## 3. Manage cookies
- On first visit you'll see a **consent banner** (per GDPR/ePrivacy).
- You can change choices any time at Footer → "Cookie preferences".
- Or disable cookies in your browser settings.

## 4. Third-party cookies
Some integrations may set their own cookies:
- **Google** (Analytics, Maps, Sign-in)
- **Stripe** (payment fraud prevention)
- **Cloudflare** (CDN, security)

Refer to their policies for details.
