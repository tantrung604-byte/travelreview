# Community Guidelines

**Version:** 1.0.0 — May 5, 2026

TravelReview is a place to share genuine travel experiences. To keep the community healthy, please follow these rules when posting reviews, photos, and comments.

## 1. Be authentic
- Only review tours you **actually took**.
- Do not accept money/gifts to write fake reviews.
- Do not create multiple accounts to boost or sink ratings.

## 2. Be respectful
- No personal attacks, racism, sexism, religious or regional discrimination.
- Do not harass guides or other travelers.
- Constructive criticism — clearly state issues, with evidence if possible.

## 3. Forbidden content
- Sexual, violent, gory, self-harm material.
- Disguised advertising, shortened links to unsafe sites.
- Other people's personal data (phone, address, ID).
- Content violating Vietnamese law (historical distortion, anti-state propaganda, etc.).

## 4. Photos & videos
- Must be **taken by you** or licensed.
- No third-party watermarks.
- No misleading edits about the location.

## 5. Enforcement
| Severity | Action |
|---|---|
| Minor (first time) | Warning + content hidden |
| Medium | Review-posting locked 7–30 days |
| Severe / repeat | Permanent account ban |
| Illegal | Reported to authorities |

## 6. Reporting
Tap ⋯ → "Report" on any review/comment/photo. Moderation responds within **24 hours**.

## 7. Appeals
If your content is removed and you believe it's a mistake, send an appeal to `appeal@travelreview.app` within **30 days**.
