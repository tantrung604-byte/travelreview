# Account Deletion

**Version:** 1.0.0 — May 5, 2026

> This page complies with Google Play (since 2024) and App Store requirements to provide an in-app **and** web-accessible account deletion mechanism.

## 1. Delete in-app

1. Open app → **Profile** tab.
2. Go to **Settings → Account → Delete account**.
3. Enter password to confirm.
4. Type `DELETE` for final confirmation.
5. Receive a confirmation email within **24 hours**.

## 2. Delete via web (without installing the app)

Visit: **https://travelreview.app/account/delete**
Sign in with the account to delete and follow instructions.

## 3. Delete via email
If you can't sign in, email `privacy@travelreview.app` from the account's email address with:
- Registered name
- Account email
- Reason (optional)

We will verify and process within **7 business days**.

## 4. What gets deleted

| Data | Action |
|---|---|
| Personal profile (name, email, avatar, phone) | **Permanently deleted** within 30 days |
| Reviews, comments, posted photos | Anonymized (content kept, name/avatar removed) or deleted per your choice |
| Wishlist, search history | Deleted immediately |
| Completed bookings | **Kept 10 years** per Accounting Law & tax obligations (mandatory, cannot delete earlier) |
| Pending bookings | Deleted after resolution (refund/voucher) |
| Payment transaction logs | Kept 10 years |
| System backups | Deleted within **90 days** per rotation cycle |

## 5. Consequences
- **Cannot be recovered** after 30 days.
- Unused coupons, points, vouchers are forfeited.
- Active bookings must be completed or cancelled per Operator policy before deletion.

## 6. Pause account (instead of delete)
If you just need a break, choose **Pause account** — data is kept and you can reactivate any time within 12 months.
