# Privacy Policy

**Effective:** May 5, 2026
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Applies to:** TravelReview app on Android, iOS and Web.

TravelReview ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This document describes how we collect, use, store and protect your personal data in compliance with Google Play, App Store, GDPR (EU), CCPA (California) and Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP (Vietnam).

## 1. Data we collect

### 1.1 Data you provide directly
- **Account info:** email, display name, avatar, optional phone number.
- **User-generated content:** reviews, comments, ratings, tour photos, wishlists.
- **Booking info:** passenger name, date of birth, passport (international tours only), emergency contact.
- **Payment:** we do **not** store card numbers. All transactions are processed by PCI-DSS compliant partners (Stripe, MoMo, VNPay).

### 1.2 Automatically collected
- **Device:** model, OS, language, timezone, advertising ID (AAID/IDFA) — only with your consent.
- **Usage:** screens viewed, actions, session duration — via Firebase Analytics.
- **Crashes:** stack traces via Firebase Crashlytics (no personal data).
- **Location:** only when you grant permission, used to suggest nearby tours. You can disable it any time in device settings.

### 1.3 From third parties
- When you sign in with Google/Apple, we receive: email, name, avatar (within scope you approve).

## 2. Purposes

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Provide booking service, display reviews | Contract performance |
| Personalize tour recommendations | Legitimate interest / Consent |
| Send transactional notifications (booking confirmation, e-tickets) | Contract performance |
| Marketing (promotions, newsletter) | **Consent** (revocable any time) |
| Analytics to improve product | Legitimate interest / Consent |
| Fraud prevention, legal compliance | Legal obligation |

## 3. Data sharing

We **do not sell** personal data. We share only with:
- **Tour operators** you book with (only data necessary to deliver the service).
- **Payment providers** (Stripe, MoMo, VNPay) — for transaction processing.
- **Infrastructure providers** (Google Firebase, Cloud Storage) — for hosting.
- **Government authorities** upon valid legal request.

## 4. Storage & Security

- Data stored on Google Cloud (`asia-southeast1` — Singapore).
- TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest.
- Passwords hashed with `bcrypt` via Firebase Authentication.
- Retention: account data kept until deletion request; transaction logs kept **10 years** per Vietnam Accounting Law.

## 5. Your rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (Settings → Download my data).
- **Correct** personal information.
- **Delete** your account permanently (see *Account Deletion*).
- **Withdraw consent** for marketing any time.
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **Lodge complaint** with the Vietnam Information Security Authority or your local data protection authority.

## 6. Children

The app is for users **13+**. Users under 18 require guardian consent before booking. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13; if we discover such data, we delete it immediately.

## 7. Cookies (Web only)

The web version uses cookies for: login session, language preference, Google Analytics. See *Cookie Policy* for details.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified in-app and by email **30 days** before taking effect.
